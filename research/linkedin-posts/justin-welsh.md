Building a "personal brand" is so 2021.

Instead, become a person worth paying attention to.

Most people struggle to come up with good content because they're not actually doing anything interesting offline.

They're walking their dog, eating dinner, going for a run, and then getting up going to work and doing it all over again.

That's gonna be tough to write about.

In 2026, the people building interesting and exciting brands are actively studying something.

They're using their innate curiosity to go deep on a topic or area that's interesting.

And when you do that every single day, you find that your brain starts thinking about what you can share:

A story
An observation
A contrarian belief
A comparison of two things
A prediction about the future
A list of helpful tools or learnings

That's why it pays to become an interesting person first, and then go try to "build a brand" (or whatever you want to call it) later.

Get curious. Study. Write. Share. Network. Grow.
