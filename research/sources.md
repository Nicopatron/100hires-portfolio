# Expert Sources — LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Topic Rationale

LinkedIn organic is the highest-leverage acquisition channel for a bootstrapped B2B SaaS with a small team. Paid channels require budget. SEO requires months. LinkedIn organic requires consistency, strategy, and zero ad spend — and the ROI compounds. These 10 experts were selected to cover a complete playbook: from understanding the algorithm to writing hooks, building content systems, measuring ROI, and distributing beyond the feed.

## Selection Criteria

- **Practitioner**: does what they teach — built an audience, generated revenue, or ran campaigns using LinkedIn organic
- **B2B focus**: not generic personal branding advice — focused on B2B SaaS, demand gen, or B2B growth
- **Active 2024–2026**: recent content reflecting current algorithm and platform dynamics
- **YouTube + LinkedIn presence**: content available on both platforms for cross-format research
- **Demonstrated results**: revenue, follower growth, or documented client outcomes
